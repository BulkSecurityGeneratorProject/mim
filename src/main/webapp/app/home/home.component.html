About

Mandela in Me is a community, communication-based platform whereby NPOs, NGOs and citizens can collaborate to communicate and meet their needs and to create awareness where the general public can donate their time, energy or money.
Mandela in Me strives to make a better world, through using the Sustainable Development Goals as the fundamental principles of our application.
Our application has streamlined the Sustainable Development Goals into 5 categories :

  1. Poverty
  2. Health
  3. Education
  4. Environment
  5. Equality
In order for the world to become a better place, we all need to lend a helping hand to people who need it most. This will bring out the Humanity in each and every one of us.
"It is in your hands to make of the world a better place."
~Nelson Mandela
